About Us

The Carl Hayden High School Marching Band is a dedicated group of musicians and performers who strive for excellence in everything they do. Our band has a long-standing tradition of excellence, and we are proud to represent our school and community with pride.

Our Mission

To provide a safe and inclusive environment for all students to learn and grow as musicians and performers.

Our Vision

To be the best high school marching band in the state of Arizona, and to represent our school and community with pride.

Our Values

We value teamwork, dedication, and excellence in everything we do. We strive to be the best we can be, and to support our fellow band members in their journey to success.
